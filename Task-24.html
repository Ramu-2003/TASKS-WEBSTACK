Travel Guide: Paris, France

About Paris

Paris is the capital of France, kown for its art, culture, and iconic landmarks. It is often called the "City of Light".

Top Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral

Travel Tips

  1. Buy a Metro pass for easy transport
  2. Learn basic French phrases
  3. Visit museums early to avoid crowds.
